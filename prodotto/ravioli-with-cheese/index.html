Category: PB Ambient Pasta

Description

Ravioli with Cheese

DOUGH (70%): durum WHEAT semolina,
pasteurized EGG mix.
FILLING (30%): ricotta cheese 37% (WHEY, MILK,
acidity regulator: citric acid E330), breadcrumbs
(soft WHEAT flour, water, salt, yeast), provolone
cheese 10.8% (MILK, salt, rennet, flavouring,
preservative E239), whey MILK, sunflower oil,
WHEAT fiber, salt, flavouring (MILK), Grana
Padano cheese 2.7% (MILK, salt, rennet,
preservative: lysozyme from EGG), potato flakes
(potatoes, emulsifier: E472), Edamer cheese
1.2% (MILK, salt, ferments, rennet), yeast extract.
Contains WHEAT, EGG, MILK.
May contain SOY and MUSTARD.
