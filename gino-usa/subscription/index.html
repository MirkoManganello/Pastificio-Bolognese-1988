Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Pasta of the Month Club subscription.

Can I skip a month or cancel my subscription?
Yes, absolutely. Through your account panel, you can pause, skip a month, or cancel your subscription at any time before the billing cycle date (the 5th of each month) with zero fees or questions asked.
Is the pasta fresh or shelf-stable?
Gino's subscription features high-quality shelf-stable stuffed tortellini, ravioli, and dried bronze-die egg pasta. Thanks to our traditional curing process, they last for up to 10 months in your pantry without refrigeration.
When are the subscription boxes shipped?
We bill on the 5th of every month and ship all subscription boxes on the 10th of the month directly from our Jacksonville, Florida warehouse. Standard ground shipping takes 2-4 business days.
Are there allergen options?
At this time, all our subscription boxes contain gluten and eggs, as they are prepared in our traditional pastificio. We hope to offer certified gluten-free options in the future.
