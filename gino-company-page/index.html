Our Mission: Authentic Italy, Made Effortless

Our mission is to bridge the gap between rich Italian culinary craftsmanship and the fast-paced modern American lifestyle. We bring the true, uncompromising flavors of Bologna directly to US homes.

We believe that a busy schedule shouldn't force you to eat artificial, sub-par food. Through innovative preservation techniques, we deliver 100% Made in Italy pantry-ready filled pasta, bakery, and specialty goods that require zero refrigeration, yet taste exactly like they were served in a historic Emilian trattoria.

FROM BOLOGNA TO THE US
OUR HERITAGE

Gino is a premium brand owned and distributed in the United States by PASTIFICIO BOLOGNESE USA INC. Born in Bologna—the ultimate global capital of filled pasta—our heritage is rooted in ancient recipes reimagined for the modern world.

Our production ecosystem spans across Italy, anchored by our proprietary manufacturing center in Bologna—the undisputed capital of filled pasta. Specializing in premium shelf-stable dry filled pasta and traditional tortellini, our Bologna facility ranks as one of the top three players in the industry. This central hub, combined with our strategic nationwide production network, ensures flawless consistency and massive capacity for both retail and global partners.

The Strength Behind the Taste

Pastificio Bolognese USA Inc. proudly operates as a direct subsidiary of Società Alimentari Italiana S.p.A. (SAI S.p.A.), a historic leader in the Italian food sector. This corporate synergy allows us to combine on-the-ground American market expertise with the immense heritage, rigorous standards, and raw material selection of our Italian parent company.

Our Uncompromising Standards

100% Made in Italy:

Every single Gino product is entirely crafted, packed, and imported directly from Italy, using premium durum wheat and certified ingredients.

Global Safety Certifications:

Our production ecosystem operates under the world's strictest food safety standards, fully certified by BRC, IFS, and ISO 14001 standards.

Shelf-Stable Convenience:

Gourmet quality that lives perfectly in your pantry. No refrigeration needed, long shelf-life, and optimized packaging.

Clean Label & R&D:

Our continuous Research & Development is dedicated to keeping ingredients clean, non-GMO, and free from unnecessary artificial preservatives.
Unwavering Commitment to Quality & Safety

SAI S.p.A. ensures an unwavering commitment to food safety through internationally recognized certifications. We are dedicated to guaranteeing the highest standards of safety, quality, and strict legal compliance in food hygiene across all our production lines.

Operating in full compliance with BRC and IFS Global Food standards, we provide our global partners and US consumers with the absolute certainty of a safe, continuously monitored, and premium-quality product.
