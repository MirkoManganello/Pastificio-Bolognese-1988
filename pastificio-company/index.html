Our Mission

Bridging Bolognese heritage and modern lifestyle

We believe the authentic taste of Bologna shouldn't be confined to a Sunday family lunch. Our mission is to carry the soul of traditional fresh sfoglia into the contemporary world. Through our advanced industrial mastery, our dry stuffed pasta preserves its rich, authentic flavor and texture with an incredible 16-month shelf life.

But we didn't stop at tradition. We are reimagining pasta for Gen Z and today’s dynamic consumers, singles and professionals who crave premium Italian quality but have little time to cook. With innovative, quick-prep formats like our Ready Rounds and Take It Easy, we deliver high-quality food experiences designed for modern, fast-paced lifestyles.

16-Month Freshness

Advanced drying technology that locks in the authentic taste of fresh pasta, with zero compromises on quality.

Contemporary Formats

Smart, quick-prep solutions designed for modern consumers who want gourmet meals in minutes.

Industrial Craftsmanship

Scaling the true Bolognese culinary heritage for global distribution, maintaining artisanal standards at high volumes.

Taking Italy Global: Meet GINO™

While Pastificio Bolognese represents our industrial and historic core, our strategic brand Gino acts as our proud ambassador across the Americas. Designed to bring the authentic, joyful Italian dining experience to international households, Gino translates our Bolognese heritage into a global language.

A Nationalwide production network

True scalability requires deep roots. To represent the authentic Italian territory and guarantee uninterrupted high-volume supply, we have built a widespread, integrated production network spanning from Veneto to Puglia.

Operating through strategic hubs across Italy, including Bassano del Grappa, Venezia, Bologna, Roma, Ferentino, and Foggia, allows us to source the best regional ingredients, mitigate supply chain risks, and deliver unparalleled manufacturing power to our global partners.
Certifications
SAI S.p.A. ensures an unwavering commitment to food safety through internationally recognized certifications. We are dedicated to guaranteeing the highest standards of safety, quality, and strict legal compliance in food hygiene across all our production lines.

Operating in full compliance with BRC and IFS Global Food standards, we provide our global partners with the absolute certainty of a safe, continuously monitored, and premium-quality product.
Corporate Governance
Our industrial success is rooted in a structured and transparent governance. S.A.I. SpA provides the financial stability, strategic vision, and corporate infrastructure that allow Pastificio Bolognese to compete at the highest levels of the global food market. From the Board of Directors to our specialized plant management, every level of our organization is aligned to guarantee excellence, scalability, and safety.
