Our Mission

Bridging Bolognese heritage and modern lifestyle

We believe the authentic taste of Bologna shouldn't be confined to a Sunday family lunch. Our mission is to carry the soul of traditional fresh sfoglia into the contemporary world.

Through our advanced industrial mastery, our dry stuffed pasta preserves its rich, authentic flavor and texture with an incredible 16-month shelf life. But we didn't stop at tradition. We are reimagining pasta for Gen Z and today’s dynamic consumers, singles and professionals who crave premium Italian quality but have little time to cook. With innovative, quick-prep formats like our Ready Rounds and Take It Easy, we deliver high-quality food experiences designed for modern, fast-paced lifestyles.

Our Roots

A Nationwide production network

True scalability requires deep roots. To represent the authentic Italian territory and guarantee uninterrupted high-volume supply, we have built a widespread, integrated production network spanning from Veneto to Puglia.

Operating through strategic hubs across Italy, including Bassano del Grappa, Venezia, Bologna, Roma, Ferentino, and Foggia, allows us to source the best regional ingredients, mitigate supply chain risks, and deliver unparalleled manufacturing power to our global partners.

Quality Standards

BRC/IFS Certified Safety

S.A.I. SpA ensures an unwavering commitment to food safety through internationally recognized certifications.

We are dedicated to guaranteeing the highest standards of safety, quality, and strict legal compliance in food hygiene across all our production lines. Operating in full compliance with BRC and IFS Global Food standards, we provide our global partners with the absolute certainty of a safe, continuously monitored, and premium-quality product.
