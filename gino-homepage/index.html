Questions, Answered

How can filled tortellini be shelf-stable without a fridge?

Our slow-drying process removes the moisture that spoilage needs while preserving the structure of the egg dough and the filling — no preservatives, no refrigeration required.

How long does delivery take, and what does shipping cost?

Orders ship from our Florida warehouse within 24 hours and arrive in 2–4 business days across the continental US. Shipping is free over $50.

How do I cook it? I’m not a chef.

You don’t need to be. Ready Rounds are done in about 5 minutes; tortellini and tortelloni go straight into boiling water for 3–4 minutes.

What if my order arrives damaged?

We’ll make it right — a replacement or a full refund, your choice. Email info@ginousa.com with a photo and your order number.
