Bolognese Roots, Global Vision

Pastificio Bolognese is the flagship brand of Società Alimentari Italiana (S.A.I. SpA). We bring together decades of Italian culinary heritage and top-tier industrial reliability to deliver uncompromising quality on a massive global scale.

Starting from the classic art of fresh sfoglia, we engineer premium dry stuffed pasta designed for modern lifestyles and dynamic Ho.Re.Ca. operations. Our innovative formats are rigorously developed to pass the 14-Minute Test, guaranteeing quick preparation, perfect texture, and an incredible 16-month shelf life without sacrificing the authentic taste of Bologna.
